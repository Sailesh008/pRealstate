## Problem Statement

> *Australian property seekers cannot easily find property prices because major portals hide them behind "contact agent" forms, wasting hours of buyer time.*



## Who This Affects

| User | Impact |
|------|--------|
| **Buyers** | Cannot compare properties by price; waste time on unaffordable homes |
| **Renters** | Face high competition; cannot quickly filter by budget |
| **Agents** | Pay high portal fees (15% yearly increase) for poor lead quality |
| **First-time buyers** | Overwhelmed by hidden pricing; no transparency |



## Why Australia Needs This

- 63% of listings hide prices – a major market failure
- First-time buyers and renters are most disadvantaged
- No major portal has made price transparency a priority
- Rental crisis requires faster, more transparent search


## Vision Statement

> *A property website where every listing displays the price upfront, saving users time and building trust.*
